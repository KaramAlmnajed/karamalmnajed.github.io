My Projects

Employee Attrition Prediction

This project utilizes machine learning to predict employee attrition. The analysis identifies key factors influencing turnover and provides actionable insights.

Methods & Tools Used:

  • Machine Learning Algorithms: Logistic Regression, Random Forest
  • Libraries: Python, Pandas, Scikit-learn, Matplotlib

Key Insights & Outcomes:

  • Turnover Rates: High turnover is associated with low job satisfaction, affecting employee retention.
  • Workload Impact: Employees facing high workloads are significantly more likely to leave the company.
  • Promotion Opportunities: Lack of promotional opportunities correlates with increased attrition rates.
